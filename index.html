OUR STORY

Family. Tradition. Legacy.

Chitter's Fritters began long before it became a business.

Growing up, Clifford spent countless hours in the kitchen with his mother learning family recipes, sharing stories, and creating memories that would last a lifetime.

One recipe always stood above the rest.

Fresh handmade Key Lime Fritters prepared with care, patience, and love.

What started as a family tradition eventually became a mission: to share a taste of Key West with the world while honoring the people and memories that inspired it.
